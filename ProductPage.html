Overview:

Show off this epic Harry Potter set in one of our high quality acrylic display cases.

Description:

Display case

  • Two tiered 10mm black high-gloss display base connected by magnets, containing embedded studs to firmly secure the entire set to the base. Plus 2 plates that fit neatly into the grid underneath the Castle, securing it in place and stopping it from moving side to side.
  • The base also features a clear information plaque displaying all the details from the set and clicks onto the base using magnets.
  • The crystal clear buildable display case is secured together using screws and connector cubes, then slots into designated holes in the base plate. This gives the product a prestigious look and ensures the case is sturdy enough to protect your valuable set. It also keeps it dust free!

Display case with printed vinyl background

  • Upgrade your display case with our in-house designed printed vinyl background sticker of a moon-lit skyline, over looking a misty valley. Inspired by the franchise to compliment this amazing collectors piece.
